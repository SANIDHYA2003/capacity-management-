Strategic Business Justification

A QSR outlet requires continuous full capacity to capture peak hourly demand. Losing a griller or cashier due to a 1-2 person staff shortage results in an immediate 15% to 35% drop in transaction speed (throughput). At peak times, this wait-time threshold drives customers to competitors, causing direct, unrecoverable revenue loss. RG deployment is the firewall that prevents labor shortfalls from translating into top-line revenue leakage.
